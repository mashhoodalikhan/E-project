About FOTOLAB

Innovative, Reliable, and Trusted in the Camera Industry Since 1991

Our Mission

At FOTOLAB, our mission is to provide unparalleled service and products in the digital camera and accessory industry. With over three decades of experience, we continue to innovate and adapt to the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Journey

1991

Company Founded

FOTOLAB was established with a vision to provide high-quality digital cameras and accessories to photography enthusiasts.

2000

Expanded Product Line

We expanded our product range to include accessories and services for SLR cameras and Handycams, meeting the growing needs of professionals and hobbyists alike.

2010

Technological Innovation

We introduced online sales and service, enabling customers to easily purchase and get expert support from the comfort of their homes.

2020

Global Reach

FOTOLAB expanded internationally, offering our products and services to customers across the globe.
